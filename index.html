What I'm working on

Currently, I'm working as a Research Fellow at the Wolfram Institute working as part of the Subchemistry team. Our current focus is to understand the combinatorics of various chemical reaction networks when we track the movement of each atom with the system compared with the standard models of chemistry. I'm also involved in theoretical computer science research on the analysis of online algorithms with Professor Nicole Wein.
